Kanaleneiland Healthy Cities — Visual Index

Five interactive HTML visualisations based on CBS OpenData Netherlands (Table 86270ENG), the WHO European Healthy Cities 6P Framework (Phase VII 2019–2024), and peer-reviewed evidence. Open each file in any modern browser — no internet connection required.

1

WHO Healthy City — Spaced Bubble Map

The original relationship map with generous spacing between 15 factor bubbles. Each bubble colour-coded by WHO 6P pillar. Toggle pillar connections, drag bubbles, click for descriptions.

Interactive · Physics simulation · 15 nodes
2

Sourced Relationship Map

Full bubble network with WHO source citations and CBS indicator scores on every node. Click any bubble to see its Kanaleneiland score, Utrecht benchmark, gap, and the academic source behind the causal connection.

Interactive · Sources visible · Gap scores
3

Index Scores Comparison Chart

Side-by-side horizontal bar chart for all 9 composite indices: Kanaleneiland vs Utrecht average. Colour-coded by WHO pillar with gap badges. Hover any row for the exact CBS survey question that produced the score.

Static chart · CBS 86270ENG data · 9 indices
4

Intervention Sequence

Six-step prioritised intervention guide grounded in WHO evidence. Click each step to expand the concrete actions for Kanaleneiland, the cascade effects, and the key indicator. Ordered: governance → economic equity → green space → loneliness → housing → active travel.

Interactive stepper · WHO sources · Cascade tags
5

Intervention to Outcome Cascade Map

Canvas diagram showing causal connections from six interventions (left) to six health outcomes (right). Line thickness encodes evidence strength. Click any bubble to highlight its specific pathways.

Interactive · Canvas · Evidence-weighted connections
